Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy Hot Wheels products?

Hot Wheels products are available worldwide at toy stores, department stores, and online retailers including our official website.

Are Hot Wheels toys safe for kids?

Yes, all Hot Wheels products comply with international safety standards and are suitable for children ages 3 and up.

Can I collect limited edition Hot Wheels?

Yes, we offer limited edition and special release cars for collectors. Check our Products page or subscribe to updates.

Do you offer warranties on your products?

Hot Wheels toys come with a manufacturer warranty covering defects. Please check the product details for specific warranty information.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support through the Contact Us page or by emailing support@hotwheels.com.
